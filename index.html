Things to Do

Summer Hot Spots

CENTRAL PARK
A fun way to spend your day. If you're feeling energetic, rent a bike for a ride around the park. For something a little more laid back, head to the Boathouse where you can rent a boat for the afternoon. Unwind with some cocktails at the Outside Bar.

THE HIGH LINE
The High Line is a public park built on a historic freight rail line elevated above the streets on Manhattan’s West Side. It runs from Gansevoort Street in the Meatpacking District to West 34th Street, between 10th and 12th Avenues and is a must-see sight. After you've walked the length, you can grab lunch at any of the many cool lunch spots in the area.

MoMA
If you're in the mood for something cultural, head over to the MoMA to catch "The Forever Now: Contemporary Paintings" exhibit. And when you're done taking in all that art, head to Terrace 5, their cafe for some delicious savory selections and wine.

www.tripadvisor.com
